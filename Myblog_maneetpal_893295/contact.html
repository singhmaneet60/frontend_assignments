In the heart of the upscale Yorkville neighborhood, you'll find Sorry Coffee Co., tucked away on a quiet street. This minimalist café is the perfect spot to enjoy a cup of specialty coffee and a freshly baked pastry while escaping the hustle and bustle of the city. Sorry Coffee Co. is nestled in the heart of Yorkville, one of Toronto's most luxurious and stylish neighborhoods. The café is a hidden gem in this upscale area, offering a tranquil escape from the bustling city streets. It's housed in a quaint and beautifully designed space that's both cozy and inviting. The café is known for its minimalist and modern interior, making it a perfect spot for a relaxing coffee break. What sets Sorry Coffee Co. apart is its dedication to crafting artisanal coffee. They take coffee seriously and source high-quality beans, often from local roasters. The baristas are skilled and passionate about their craft, ensuring that every cup of coffee is a work of art. Whether you prefer a classic espresso, a creamy latte, or a pour-over, you'll find the perfect coffee to suit your taste.
