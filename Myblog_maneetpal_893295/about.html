Or explore the depths of Rasta Pasta for a fusion of Caribbean and Italian flavors that will transport your taste buds to a tropical paradise. Kensington Market is, without a doubt, a food lover's paradise. The neighborhood is home to a wide range of international cuisine, from Mexican and Caribbean to Chinese and Middle Eastern. Here are some of the hidden food gems within Kensington Market: Seven Lives Tacos Y Mariscos: If you're a fan of seafood, you can't miss this tiny taco shop. Their Baja-style fish tacos are legendary. Rasta Pasta: As mentioned earlier, this place fuses Caribbean and Italian flavors, offering dishes like jerk chicken pasta and curry shrimp linguine that are bursting with unique tastes. Dirty Bird: If you're into fried chicken, Dirty Bird is a must-visit. Their crispy fried chicken and inventive toppings are a mouthwatering experience. Wanda's Pie in the Sky: For dessert, you can't go wrong with Wanda's. This bakery offers a mouthwatering array of pies, tarts, and cakes that will satisfy any sweet tooth.
